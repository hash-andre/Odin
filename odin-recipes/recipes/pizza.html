Pizza is one of the most popular Italian dishes in the world. It is made with a simple dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and different toppings.

This recipe is easy to customize. You can keep it simple with mozzarella and tomato sauce, or add your favorite ingredients.

Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Your favorite toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to a high temperature.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Drizzle a little olive oil on top.
  6. Bake the pizza until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Add fresh basil before serving.
  8. Slice and enjoy.
