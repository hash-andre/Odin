Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with ladyfingers, coffee, mascarpone cream, and cocoa powder. It is soft, creamy, and full of flavor.

This dessert does not need to be baked. It is prepared in layers and then chilled in the fridge before serving.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Coffee
  • Cocoa powder
  • Dark chocolate flakes

Steps

  1. Prepare the coffee and let it cool.
  2. Separate the egg yolks from the egg whites.
  3. Mix the egg yolks with sugar until creamy.
  4. Add the mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth.
  5. Beat the egg whites and gently fold them into the cream.
  6. Dip the ladyfingers quickly into the coffee.
  7. Place a layer of ladyfingers in a dish.
  8. Add a layer of mascarpone cream.
  9. Repeat the layers.
  10. Dust the top with cocoa powder.
  11. Chill in the fridge before serving.
