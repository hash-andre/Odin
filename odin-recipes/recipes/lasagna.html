Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and cheese. It is baked in the oven until the top becomes golden and the layers are rich and flavorful.

This recipe is perfect for a family meal or a special dinner. It takes some time to prepare, but the result is worth it.

Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the onion, carrot, and celery.
  2. Heat some olive oil in a pan and cook the vegetables for a few minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, salt, and pepper, then let the sauce simmer.
  5. Spread a layer of sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
  7. Add béchamel sauce, meat sauce, and cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  9. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  10. Bake in the oven until the lasagna is golden and bubbling.
  11. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
